The Team Behind Studera

We are two students who experienced the broken college counselling system firsthand — and decided to build something better.

Why We Built This

Too many brilliant students miss their dream university not because they are not good enough, but because they never had access to the right guidance. Studera exists to change that — one mentor match at a time.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide everything we build at Studera.

🎯

Precision Over Generics

Every student deserves guidance that is specific to their university, programme, country, and background — not a one-size-fits-all approach.

🤝

Peer-to-Peer Trust

The most credible advice comes from someone who has already walked your exact path. We connect students with mentors who truly understand.

🌍

Global Accessibility

Great guidance should not be limited to students who can afford expensive counsellors. Studera makes world-class mentorship accessible to all.

📊

Meritocracy

Our mentors are selected through a strict, data-driven process. GPA, grades, scholarships, and a statement of interest — only the best make the cut.
